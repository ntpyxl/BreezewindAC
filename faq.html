Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Quick answers related to our products services.

General Questions

How often should I service my AC?

It's recommended to service your AC at least once a year to maintain efficiency and prevent breakdowns.

What size AC unit do I need for my space?

The correct AC size depends on your room's square footage, insulation, and heat sources. A professional assessment ensures the best fit.

How long do air conditioning units last?

A well-maintained AC unit typically lasts between 10-15 years. Regular servicing can extend its lifespan.

What's the best temperature setting for my AC?

For energy savings, set your thermostat to around 24-26°C (75-78°F) when at home and a bit higher when away or just simply turn it off.

Maintenance & Energy Efficiency

How can I improve my AC's energy efficiency?

Keep air filters clean, schedule regular maintenance, seal air leaks, and use a programmable thermostat.

Why is my AC leaking water?

Common causes include clogged drain lines, dirty air filters, or low refrigerant levels. A technician can diagnose and fix the issue.

How do I know if my AC needs a refrigerant recharge?

Signs include weak cooling, ice buildup on coils, or hissing noises from the unit.

Why does my AC emit a bad smell?

Mold growth, clogged filters, or a burnt-out motor can cause unpleasant odors. Regular cleaning helps prevent this issue.

Installation & Repairs

How long does it take to install an AC unit?

Installation typically takes between 2-8 hours, depending on the unit type and complexity of the setup.

What should I do if my AC stops working suddenly?

Check your thermostat, circuit breaker, and air filters. If the problem persists, contact a professional technician.

Can I repair my AC unit myself?

Basic maintenance like filter cleaning can be done at home, but complex repairs require professional expertise to avoid damage or voiding warranties.
